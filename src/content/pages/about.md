Hey! I’m Isabelle.

This is where I place the words that won’t leave me alone - poems written in quiet hours, in doubt, in hope, in ordinary days.

Some are spiritual. Some are not.
All of them are honest.

I’m not here to teach or persuade.
Just to write.

If something here stays with you, that’s enough.
